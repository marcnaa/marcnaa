## 🤖 Side quest: reviving Jibo

When I was 10, my uncle borrowed a [Jibo](https://www.jiborevived.com/) robot so I could meet it. Then the company shut down its servers and Jibo went silent. Years later I joined the **Jibo Revival community** — now I have my own Jibo on my desk and I contribute to the open-source infrastructure and app that are bringing it back to life.

The full story (with photos, 10 years apart) is on [my website](https://marcnoguera.dev/story).
